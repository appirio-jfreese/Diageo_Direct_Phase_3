Bulleit Bourbon F13 H2
Distribution 7/1/2012 - 7/31/2012 Back

Consumer behavior is showing trends toward premiumization. Wines priced at $10 and above are outperforming the overall wine market (+15%); over the last 52 weeks, dollar growth for the $10-$15 (+12%) and $15-$25 (+18%) segments have significantly outpaced the $5-$7 (+2%) and $7-$10 (+5%) segments.

More people are having more drinking occasions at home, and these changes are here to stay: 60% cooking more at home, 26% getting together more with friends. 53% said they will continue to cook more at home.

Consumers are often turning to celebrity and local chefs for wine and food pairing ideas and techniques and brand recommendations, as evident by the popularity of cooking shows and wine segments on cooking shows.
